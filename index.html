About the Website

Project Finder is an innovative platform designed specifically for high school and middle school students, welcoming individuals from all backgrounds. The website provides a space for both juniors and seniors to discover or create passion projects. It encourages students to explore a range of extracurricular opportunities, develop leadership skills, and pursue their interests. With an emphasis on personal growth and community involvement, Project Finder aims to empower teens to take charge of their futures by joining or starting projects that ignite their passions.

Website Founder

Maryam Nawaz is the founder of Project Finder, a platform she created to help students discover their passions, work with others, and build skills that will help them in school and life. Maryam is passionate about empowering teens and giving them the tools to grow personally and academically. With Project Finder, her goal is to create a space where students can lead projects that truly matter to them, while learning to work together, be creative, and develop their leadership skills.
